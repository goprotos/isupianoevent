About This Event

Hosted in partnership with Idaho State University

This piano event is held on the Idaho State University campus at the Stephens Performing Arts Center, an Idaho State University facility. Pianos previously used by the ISU Department of Music are being offered to the public, with proceeds supporting the continued operation of the university's institutional piano loan program.

Event Hosted By
Piano Gallery Music Superstore
Idaho State University's Authorized Retail Partner

ISU has a special arrangement with Yamaha, Kawai, and Piano Gallery Music Superstore that benefits the university's music students and faculty. The institutional loan program brings high-quality instruments onto campus for student use, with inventory rotated periodically by offering pianos to the public at substantially reduced pricing.

About this event: Piano Gallery Music Superstore is the seller of these instruments. Idaho State University is the institution loaning the pianos and provides the on-campus venue. ISU does not directly sell, endorse, or sponsor this advertisement.

✦ The Official Letter ✦

From the Idaho State University Music Department

Official ISU Music Department Letter

Dear Friends,

We are pleased to notify you that many fine pianos loaned to the music department at Idaho State University are now available for purchase. This letter allows you to purchase a piano before the public sale. Pianos in the loan program have additional discounts and include a factory warranty.

ISU has a special arrangement with Yamaha, Kawai, and Piano Gallery that directly benefits our talented music students and faculty. We need your support to continue this program.

In addition to the institutional pianos, a selection of new and pre-owned pianos will be available for purchase at a fraction of retail. New and used Yamaha, Steinway, Bösendorfer, German-built Schimmel pianos, Yamaha Disklavier player pianos, Yamaha Clavinova digital pianos, Seiler technology-driven hybrid pianos, and PianoDisc & QRS player piano systems — all substantially discounted.

Over 100 pianos under one roof for you to play and purchase.

Two ways to select and purchase a piano:

By Appointment Only: Wednesday and Thursday, May 13th and 14th. Arrange an appointment by calling (208) 206-5653 or booking online below.

General Public Sale: Friday, May 15th from 10am to 8pm and Saturday, May 16th from 10am to 2pm at the Stephens Performing Arts Center on the ISU campus in Pocatello.

— Idaho State University Music Department
